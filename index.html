We've got what you need!

Users can choose how many virtually credits they wish to place on the bet. A fraction of the total sum of credits placed on a bet will be distributed amongst the winners. A bet could be similar to “Will the weather in [location] at [time] be greater or less than [specific temperature]”. Another example of a bet could also be, “Which location, [location A] or [location B], will have a higher temperature at [time] tomorrow.” We will collect weather data to validate the outcome of bets, as well as choose thresholds for the bets!
